INTERNSHIP @ CODEGURUKUL
June 2015

DESCRIPTION

The CodeGurukul Internship Program is a one month program where we train and equip you on various technologires for two weeks followed by two weeks of hands on industry simulated project experience.



OVERVIEW

WEEK ONE:

Foundation Course

Refresher course on HTML5, CSS3 and Javascript which will get your creative juices flowing! Ignite the designer in you!

Portfolio time!!

Build your very own website by harnessing the power of Bootstrap!

Social Coding

Get into the world of version control using Git.

WEEK TWO:

Enter the MEAN Stack

Dive into the complexities of creating a full blown web application using MongoDB, Express, AngularJS and Node.js which is taking over the web!

Hosting

So you can't host. And you call yourself a pro. Buckle up and understand the basics of deploying your app to the cloud.

WEEK THREE AND FOUR:

So you think you can code?

This is when we let the reigns loose, set you free and let you work on some good projects that will leave you wanting for more. This project is compulsory in order to for you to receive the the internship letter.
At the end of this workshop, you will be ready to take on any web interview that comes your way!
