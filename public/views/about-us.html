About Us

Our company background

Codegurukul trains passionate and creative people in web and mobile development. We bring together people with diverse perspectives and backgrounds who are passionate about technology,from investment bankers and spinal surgeons to cartoonists and sky-diving instructors. Codegurukul graduates now work as software developers in top notch tech companies.

The duration of our program is 12 weeks, full-time, and we train people for careers in software development. We partner with Codeniti to launch the Codeniti Fellowship Program and provide free training to under-represented groups in the tech community. We also offer part-time courses, corporate training, and a Pre-College Program for high school students.
