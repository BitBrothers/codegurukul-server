Course Overview

The Fresher to Hacker will train students to build web applications. Users don’t want to download a native iOS or Android app to take care of day to day activities on the web; they want highly-interactive websites. This has caused a boom in interest amongst companies looking to build client-side (read: mobile-friendly) web-apps that consume a JSON API rather than rely on kilobyte-heavy server-side rendering.

The JavaScript landscape is changing almost weekly, with new libraries and techniques released by both major companies and independent, open-source developers. The result? Developers who understand emerging best practices are able to put up fully-featured and scalable apps in less time.
